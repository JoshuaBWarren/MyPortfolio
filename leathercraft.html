Leather carving: Bulbasaur

Leather Carving

Project description

I enjoy going out to events where I can dress up like a Viking so it only made sense that I get started on a Viking war belt project!

This is a belt with an 8 oz base and 4 to 5 ounce individual pieces on top. Each of the 5 pieces are veg tan leather. Holding them onto the base are brass rivets. 2 to 3 oz leather straps keep it together.

I thoroughly enjoyed this project and may do several more like it as I branch out and learn more about the craft!

Leather carving: Spirit Wolf Pouch

Leather Carving

Project description

This was part of my Viking outfit project and what Viking goes into battle to raid and pillage without a bag to hold all the gold in?!

This pouch is tooled 3 oz veg tan leather. The design has a bit of green dye on the head stone. It's stitched together with deer skin leather on the side for some elasticity. It has two loops to accomodate a moderately sized belt with a small leather lace to allow the wearer to secure the flap.

The best part of this project was the tooling. I honestly didn't see it coming out this good until it was done. I'm very proud of the work.
